
# 📌 Prerequisites

## ⚠️ Things to Check:

- 🪪 SIM card: Older SIMs might not support it — ask your carrier for an upgrade (usually free).
- ⬆️ Software update: Make sure your phone is on the latest version.
- 📶 Plan/Account settings: Sometimes carriers need to activate the feature on your account first.

## ✨ What you'll need:
1. Wifi calling capable Android device or iPhone (iOS 12 and up)
2. A Registered Smart Sim Card 
3. Wifi Connection
4. At least a single bar of mobile signal to receive an OTP text message from smart
5. Messenger App installed - needed to chat smart customer service
